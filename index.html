How to buy

Create a Wallet:

Download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.app.

Get Some SOL:

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $PEPINU. If you don’t have any SOL, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium:

Connect to Raydium. Connect your wallet. Paste the $PEPINU token address into Raydium, select Pepinu, and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch SOL for $PEPINU:

Switch SOL for $PEPINU. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
